This game tests your memory and concentration. Have fun playing and challenging yourself to reach the highest level!

Rules:

1. Start the Game:
The game only begins when you press a key.

2. Follow the Sequence:
Carefully observe the order of the buttons flashing and replicate the exact order during your turn.

3. No Skipping:
You must click every button in the sequence, one at a time.

4. Be Precise:
If you make a mistake, the game ends, and your score will be based on the last successfully completed level.

5. Progressive Difficulty:
With each level, the sequence grows longer, making it more challenging to remember.
